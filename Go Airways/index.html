About Go Airways

Welcome! As always, great storytelling starts with the ‘when’. Our story began in 2013 as an ambitious venture between Go! AIrways and Aeroworld. We are a young, aspirational airline that has grown significantly in the last 8 years. We are the #1* on-time performance airline in India. How? Innovation. Leveraging technology helped us champion dreams and seize opportunities to enrich the travel experience for our guests. We launched with the underlying premise that a great travel experience translates to happy guests and in turn, leads to a healthier business. Our domestic network has been growing stronger and we’ve expanded into key metro cities. We operate with the capacity to serve over a million guests every month, connecting 18 destinations across the country.

Innovation sits atop everything we do. We leverage data, machine learning, and artificial intelligence to change how we run our business, through digital transformation. We aim to move beyond being just an airline, to meet the demands of an increasingly digital lifestyle. Our time in the aviation industry has reinforced our belief that a great flying experience must be made accessible to everyone. Being a brand that embraces technology to enhance the customer experience, we’ve launched multiple offerings.

Our Customer Happiness support team exhibits an exceptional turnaround time for query resolution. Our multi-tasking chatbot, Tia, answers your queries in English and Hindi, and can help resolve your problems via WhatsApp as well. We also have an extensive inventory of fare products and services, and an agency network of 2500+ partners. Plush leather seats, a wide choice of hot meals with an extensive in-flight menu, exclusive benefits on airasia.co.in – all these and more without being heavy on one’s pocket.
